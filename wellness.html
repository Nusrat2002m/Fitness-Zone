Wellness

Wellness is a holistic concept that encompasses the overall well-being of an individual, integrating various dimensions of health to achieve a balanced and fulfilling life. It goes beyond mere physical health to include mental, emotional, social, spiritual, environmental, and financial aspects. Wellness is an active process of making conscious choices toward a healthy and fulfilling life. There are some types of Wellness.These are:

  • 1. Physical Wellness Practices

  • 2. Mental and Emotional Wellness Practices

  • 3. Social Wellness Practices
  • 4. Occupational Wellness Practices

  • 5. Spiritual Wellness Practices

  • 6. Environmental Wellness Practices

  • 7. Financial Wellness Practices

  • 8. Intellectual Wellness Practices

  • 9. Recreational Wellness Practices
